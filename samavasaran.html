SAMAVASARAN

Sittanavasal Cave (Arivar Koil) Paintings are located in Pudukkottai town in Tamil Nadu. Its paintings are based on the theme of Jain Samava-Sarana (Preaching Hall or Audience Hall where Jain Tirthankaras delivered sermons after they reached realization – kevalagnana).
